! shoyu tare

#tare
combine in medium saucepan
  1   | cup      | soy sauce
  1/2 | cup      | sake
  1/2 | cup      | mirin
  1   | tbsp     | brown sugar
  2   | cloves   | garlic [smashed]
  1   | 2" piece | ginger [peeled, coined]

simmer #tare over medium heat for 00:10:00
strain/filter #tare into bowl
