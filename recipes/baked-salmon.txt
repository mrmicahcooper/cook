! baked salmon

#marinade
mix in a medium bowl
  2 | cloves | garlic
  6 | tbsp   | olive oil
  1 | tsp    | dried basil
  1 | tsp    | salt
  1 | tsp    | black pepper [ground]
  1 | tbsp   | lemon juice
  1 | tbsp   | fresh parsley [chopped]

#fish
pour #marinaded on top of 
  2-4 | each | salmon

let sit for 01:00:00

wrap each #fish in aluminum foil
back #fish @ 350-360 for 00:35:00 - 00:40:00
