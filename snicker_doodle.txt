! snicker doodle

#cream mix
cream together
  1/2 | cup | softened butter
  1/2 | cup | shortening
  1/2 | cup | white sugar
  2   |     | eggs
  2   | tsp | vanila extract

#dough
blend in
  2 3/4 | cup  | all purpose flour
  2     | tsp. | cream of tartar
  1     | tsp  | baking soda
  1/4   | tsp  | salt

#doughballs
roll #dough into balls

roll #doughballs in
  2 | tbsp | sugar
  2 | tsp  | cinnamon

Place #doughballs 2 inches apart on ungreased pan

Bake #doughballs @ 400 for 00:08:00 - 00:10:00
