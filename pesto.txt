! pesto

#greens
pulse in food processor until roughly chopped
  1   | clove | garlic
  2/3 | cup   | toasted walnut
  2   | cup   | fresh basil [loosely packed]
pour in and pulse until combined
  1/2 | cup   | extra-virgin olive oil

#juices
combine in a large bowl
  #greens
  2/3 | cup   | pecorino romaono cheese [grated]
  4   | oz    | goat cheese [crumbled]
  1   | whole | lemon zest
  1   | whole | lemon juice
