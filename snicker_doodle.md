# Snicker Doodle
```
Cream together
 1/2 cup -> softened butter
 1/2 cup -> shortening
 1/2 cup -> white sugar
 2 -> eggs
 2 tsp. vanila extract
Blend in
 2 3/4 cup -> all purpose flour 
 2 tsp. -> cream of tartar
 1 tsp -> baking soda
 1/4 tsp -> salt
Roll into balls
Roll in
 2 tbsp. -> sugar
 2 tsp -> cinnamon
Place
 2 inches apart on ungreased pan
Bake @ 400 for 00:08:00 - 00:10:00
```
