! smothered porkchops

#flour
add to flat dish
  1 | cup | flour

#eggs
add in flat dish
  2 | each | eggs

#chops
dip in #flour then #eggs then #flour
  6 | each | porkchops

deep fry #chops

#gravy
brown in a large skillet
  1   | each | bell pepper [chopped]
  1/2 | each | onion [large, chopped]
  3   | tbsp | flour

add to the #gravy
 3 | cups | water
 1 | pack | brown gravy

simmer #chops in #gravy for 00:20:00
